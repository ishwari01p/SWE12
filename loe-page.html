Leadership

SWE CCEW organizes network mixers and mentorship programs for the students of the college to interact with each other and industry experts to develop professional and personal networks. Such events help the students to develop their leadership skills and gain insight into the working of the industry. The planning and execution of such events helps the student representatives and panel members gain an insight as to how large-scale events are planned and how things are handled professionally within the industry. It helps them to understand the importance of teamwork and develop their leadership skills and communication skills.

Engineering

SWE CCEW organizes events like the Incube conference to empower the engineers of tomorrow, to understand the problems society faces today and to come up with innovative solutions for the same. These events bring together bright minds including strong engineering leaders from across the industry and students to talk about topics related to the theme. The aim is to provoke conversations that matter and foster learning, leadership, motivation, and inclusion.

Outreach
